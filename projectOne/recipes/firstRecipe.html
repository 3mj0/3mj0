Julius's Fav Dish

Description

This is double bacon and beef stew, my oldest son's favourite meal I've made ever. (so far)

It warms the heart and soothes the soul because I use a gravy base and cook it with love.

Perfect "under a blanky with a movie type dish!

Ingredients

  • Beef Cubes
  • Beef Broth
  • Smokey Bacon
  • Thick Cut Peppered Bacon
  • Carrots
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic
  • Gravy
  • Seasonings
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Press the garlic and green onion into the pan and brown them
  2. Add beef chunks and stir occasionally adding spices and seasoning till the ancestors tell you to stop
  3. Fry the two different types of bacon in a seperate pan while cuttin carrots up to boil
  4. Add gravy and beef broth to the pan with the garlic and cubes to start the heating
  5. Drain and rinse the carrots, add them to the gravy mix
  6. Break up the crispy bacon and crumble it into the stew
  7. Let stew for another 15 minutes and serve with some grated cheese or cripsy onions
  8. Pairs well with a light citrusy white wine and hugs.
